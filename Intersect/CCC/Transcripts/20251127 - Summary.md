<summary><strong>Decisions/Actions</strong></summary>

### Decisions Made

* Withhold Retroactive Compensation: The voting members passed a motion to withhold the retroactive service rewards (stipends) for the period of July to November from members due to  confirmed inactivity and non-contribution to the Civics Committee's activities (no meeting attendance, working group participation, or Discord activity).

### &#x20;Key Actions & Updates

* Snap Election Timeline Acceleration: The voting for the CC Snap Election was opened sooner than initially planned (on November 26th instead of December 1st) to give D-Reps more time to vote.
* Next Steps: Voting closes on December 5th. The on-chain governance action is scheduled for submission on Monday, December 8th, following internal and third-party audits.
* Member Compensation Policy Implementation: Intersect is moving forward with the bulk payment of member compensation, backdated from June 2025 (5 months/3,000 ADA).
* Next Steps: For future payments starting in January, members will self-claim rewards via the member area, requiring a short summary of their contributions. The committee decided to review and potentially publish the attendance tracker for transparency.
* Vice Chair Election: The committee formally initiated the process to elect a Vice Chair in December. Members are encouraged to consider and announce their intent in the coming weeks.
* Annual Members Meeting (AMM) Participation: Members were strongly encouraged to attend the Intersect AMM on Saturday, November 29th, specifically the dedicated Civics Session at 10:00 AM UTC in Room 1.
* Civics Committee Charter Review: The Charter was reviewed, and it was noted that the broad scope should be kept in mind. Members were encouraged to study the Charter and consider making amendment suggestions in the future.
* Working Group Update Postponement: The planned updates from the working groups (including a presentation by Gintama) were postponed until the next meeting due to the extended discussion on member compensation.
