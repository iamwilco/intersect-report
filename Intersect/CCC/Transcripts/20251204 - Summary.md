  #### &#x20;**Decisions Made (Votes)**

  * Approve Governance Health Working Group (GHWG) Report for Publication: The committee approved the final GHWG report, including its identified KPIs, for publication.
  * Condition: Publication is scheduled for after the December 11th meeting, allowing all voting members one week to review the 24-page document for any major errors or issues before final release.
  * Approve Constitutional Amendment Process (CAP) Requirements Document: The committee approved the CAP Requirements Document (Phase 1 deliverable).
  * Result: This approval allows the working group to move immediately into Phase 2 (building out the constitutional amendment process).

  #### Key Actions & Updates

  * CC Snap Election Final Push: Members were urged to use social media and personal accounts to make a final push for D-Rep participation before the voting closed on December 5th.
  * Update: Voting closes on December 5th at 12:00 UTC. Results, subject to audit, will be announced shortly thereafter, with the on-chain governance action planned for Monday, December 8th.
  * Encourage On-Chain Participation: A general action item to begin preparing messaging to encourage high D-Rep and SPO participation in the upcoming on-chain vote (following the snap election).
  * GHWG Standardized Measurement Proposal: An action was logged for the GHWG to take up the suggestion to develop standardized measurement methodologies/queries for the KPIs in the future (Phase 2), ensuring consistent data across the ecosystem.
  * Governance Education Working Group Scheduling Fix: LM was assigned the action to investigate and resolve the scheduling issue that caused the Governance Education working group to miss recent meetings.

  Constitution Discussion Continuation: The complex and philosophical discussion initiated by G on the legal status, naming, and enforcement authority of the Cardano Constitution was determined to require more time and was deferred to the working group channels (potentially the CAP working group) and future committee sessions.

  * AMM Review: The success of the Annual Members Meeting (AMM) in generating interest (e.g., a new member for the CAP working group) was noted, and an action was logged to brainstorm ideas on how to better utilize the Interverse space for Civics Committee outreach and understanding.
