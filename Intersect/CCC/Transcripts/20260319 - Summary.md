## Decisions/Actions

The CIVICS Committee meeting covered updates on partner chain governance and technical hard fork status. Key discussions included finalizing working group deliverables and a significant recommendation to reduce the Constitutional Committee (CC) minimum size from seven to five to enhance governance resilience. Consensus was reached to proceed with this recommendation, while emphasizing a desired actual committee size of seven or more members.

### Decisions Made

Constitutional Committee Minimum Size Reduction: Consensus was confirmed to move forward with the recommendation from the Parameter Committee and TSC to reduce the Constitutional Committee (CC) minimum size from 7 to 5. This aims to prevent governance stalling due to a single resignation, with the understanding that the desired actual committee size should remain 7 or more.

CAP Editors' Roles and Guidelines Ratification: The CAP working group finalized CAP editors' roles and guidelines, which have met quorum in favor of ratification and are expected to be ratified next week.
