## Decisions/Actions

**Decisions**

* **Approval of Proof of Concept (PoC):** The committee decided to pursue a small PoC for the "Marketing DAO" with a limited budget for this year rather than a full integration.
* **Travel Budget Parameters:** Agreed on a budget framework for 3–5 events, covering a maximum of 5 people per event, with specific caps for tickets ($3,000) and accommodation ($250/night).
* **Summit Location & Strategy:** Confirmed the 2024 Cardano Summit will be held in Singapore (October 5–6) to coincide with Token 2049 to consolidate travel and maximize attendance.
* **Treasury Refund Policy:** Agreed that any unused travel funds from the requested budget will be returned to the Cardano Treasury.
* **Submission Timeline:** Set an internal deadline of April 10th to have the budget document finalized and triple-checked.

**Actions**

* **Lara:** Review and increase the travel budget (potentially up to $25k) to account for the projected 60% increase in aviation fuel costs.
* **Lara:** Check with other committees and Intersect for an official ADA/USD conversion rate to use in the proposal.
* **Laura:** Reach out to Logan to inform him the committee is looking at a PoC model with a limited budget for the marketing execution arm.
* **Laura:** Contact Sam (Product Committee) to schedule a meeting regarding overlapping Business Development (BD) and growth initiatives.
* **Lara:** Consult with Lorenzo (Head of Product) about creating a visible space on the Intersect website or Discord to map all committee roadmaps.
* **Committee Members:** Review the shared proposal document on Discord, specifically the sections highlighted for "KPI/Metric" and "Section 2: Alignment to Strategy."
* **Musa:** Provide information to the committee regarding major blockchain events in Africa to ensure regional inclusivity in future marketing plans.
