## Overall Leadership Assessment

### Is the committee well-led? NO

The committee suffers from:
1. **Weak facilitation** leading to unproductive meetings
2. **Role confusion** between elected members and secretary
3. **Poor conflict resolution** mechanisms
4. **Inconsistent decision-making** processes
5. **Communication silos** (WhatsApp groups, side meetings)

### Biggest Leadership Failure

**Failure to establish clear operational procedures and enforce them consistently**

Evidence:
- Multiple platform debates (Discord vs WhatsApp vs ClickUp)
- Voting procedure confusion (live votes vs polls)
- Access and tooling issues persisting for months
- Side channels undermining official communication

### Recommendations for Change

1. **Establish written operating procedures** covering:
   - Decision-making protocols
   - Communication channels
   - Tool usage requirements
   - Meeting management rules

2. **Enforce secretary boundaries**:
   - Limit to administrative functions
   - No vote advocacy
   - Guest invitations require committee approval

3. **Improve meeting discipline**:
   - Strict agenda adherence
   - Time limits on discussions
   - Action item tracking

4. **Resolve tooling issues permanently**:
   - Single source of truth for tasks
   - Clear access permissions
   - Documented workflows

The committee needs stronger leadership discipline and clearer operational structure to function effectively.
